AI Decision Service Demo

This is a simple demo of reinforcement learning for news recommendation. The AI decides which news headline to show based on information about the website visitor. The user then gives feedback about the relevancy of the item. After a while, the AI will learn to suggest the most relevant item to each user. From time to time, it suggests something different, in order to learn more.

The underlying technology isn't just for news: you can it use to personalise UI items and placements, optimise pricing, automatically assign customer support tickets to people, and much more.
